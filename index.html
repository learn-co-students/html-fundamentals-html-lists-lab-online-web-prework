Kellye's Grilled Cheese Sandwich

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 1tbsp of butter
  • 4 slices of cheese
    • cheddar
    • mozarella
    • pepper jack
  1. Spread buter on bread and frying pan
  2. Place bread in frying pan and fry
  3. Add cheese on top of bread
  4. Cover with second slice of bread
  5. Turn over and fry for 2 minutes
